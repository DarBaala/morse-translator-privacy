Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Morse Translator ("the App") is developed by nmteg app's. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No data is sent to any server

Local Storage

The App saves your preferences (theme, language, alphabet) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Internet Access

The App works entirely offline. No internet connection is required or used.

Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera — used solely to control the flashlight (torch) for Morse code signaling. The App does not capture photos, record video, or access the camera image in any way.
  • Audio — used to play Morse code sounds locally on your device. No audio is recorded.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
